\# Phishing Risk Assessment



\## Overall Risk Rating



\*\*High\*\*



\## Risk Factors



| Factor | Assessment |

|---|---|

| Brand impersonation | High |

| Sender-domain mismatch | High |

| Reply-To mismatch | High |

| Suspicious link | High |

| Urgency and threatening language | Medium |

| Generic greeting | Medium |

| Attachment present | No |

| User interaction required | Yes |



\## Impact



If a user followed the link and submitted credentials, potential consequences could include:



\- Account compromise

\- Unauthorised mailbox access

\- Credential reuse against other services

\- Internal phishing from the compromised account

\- Data exposure

\- Further social-engineering attacks



\## Recommended Response



\- Quarantine the message

\- Block the sender and related domains

\- Search for similar messages across mailboxes

\- Review URL-click telemetry

\- Reset credentials if the link was accessed

\- Revoke active sessions where compromise is suspected

\- Report the incident to the security team
